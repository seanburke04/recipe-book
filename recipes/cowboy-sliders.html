Cowboy Sliders

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion (diced)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Seasoned Salt
  • 1 tbsp Hamburger Seasoning
  • 1 Jalapeno Pepper (sliced)
  • 6 slices Thick-cut Bacon (crumbled)
  • 1 cup French's Crispy Fried Onions
  • 1 pkg King's Hawaiian Rolls
  • Cowboy Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly grease baking dish.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat add oil and cook onion and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, and hamburger seasoning. Remove grease while cooking.
  4. Split rolls and place bottoms in baking dish.
  5. Pour 2 tbsp of cowboy butter over rolls in the baking dish.
  6. Top with hamburger mixture.
  7. Distribute jalapenos, bacon, shredded cheese, and crispy fried onions on the sliders.
  8. Place top halves of the rolls on the sliders and and brush with melted cowboy butter.
  9. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove foil and bake for an additional 3-5 minutes or until the tops turn golden brown.

Notes

  • Can substitue cowboy butter with garlic butter.
