Spring Onion Dip

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs. Spring Onions (about 4 bunches)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1.25 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 4 tbsp Salted Butter
  • 5 oz. Room Temperature Cream Cheese
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 6 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Position an oven rack about 6 inches below the broiler, then set the broiler to high.
  2. Cut spring onions to seperate whites and greens. Place greens on a baking sheet, toss with olive oil and 1/4 tsp salt.
  3. Broil for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Thinly slice whites of onions. Melt butter in a large saute pan over medium heat. Add onion whites, and 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Cook stirring occasionally, until soft, and jammy, about 30 minutes.
  6. In a bowl beat cream cheese until smooth. Mis in sour cream, mayonnaise , and remaining 3/4 tsp salt.
  7. Thinly slice cooled greens and stir them in the cream cheese mixture. Stir in the caramelized onions, and pepper. Sprinkle top with paprika and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  8. Let sit at room temperature for at least 15 minutes before serving.

Notes

  • If dark bits form at the bottom of the pan while cooking the onions, add 1/4 cup of water and scrape pan. Repeat as needed.
