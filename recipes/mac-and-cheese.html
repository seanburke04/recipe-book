Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Elbow Macaroni, Cavatappi, or other pasta
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 6 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/3 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 3 cups Whole Milk
  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 4 cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
  • 2 cups Gruyere Cheese (shredded)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1.5 cups Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 4 tbsp Butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese (shredded)
  • 1/4 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 lb. Thick cut Bacon (optional)
  • 1 bag Frozen Peas (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and lightly grease a large baking dish.
  2. Cook bacon and set aside.
  3. Combine cheddar and Gruyere cheeses.
  4. Cook pasta one minute under al dente. Drain, drizzle with olive oil, and stir.
  5. Melt unsalted butter in a large dutch oven or stock pot.
  6. Whisk in flour over medium heat until golden.
  7. Gradually whisk in the milk and heavy cream until smooth. Continue whisking until 2 minutes after bubbles appear.
  8. Add two cups of the shredded cheese mixture and whisk until smooth. Repeat with another two cups.
  9. Stir in pasta, bacon, and peas (if adding).
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Pour half the mac and cheese into the baking dish and top with remaining cheese mixture before adding the remaining mac and cheese.
  12. In a bowl combine melted butter, Panko bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, and paprika. Sprinkle over mac and cheese.
  13. Bake until golden brown, about 30 minutes and serve.
