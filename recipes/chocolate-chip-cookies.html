Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Salted Butter (softened)
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar (packed)
  • 2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Mix together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients.
  6. Mix in chocolate chips.
  7. Roll dough balls about 2-3 tablespoons in size and space them evenly on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they just barely start to brown.
  9. Let sit on baking sheet for 5 minutes before moving them to cooling rack.

Notes

  • Can be frozen for up to 2 months. Roll into balls before freezing.
  • Can be baked from frozen or thawed first.
  • If baking from frozen preheat to 350F and put the cookies in while oven preheats. Set the timer for 15 minutes.
