Cowboy Butter

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 tbsp coarse Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp fresh Chives (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh Thyme
  • 2 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1/4 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Crushed Red Pepper Flakes

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients.

Notes

  • Lasts up to 5 days in the fridge
  • Can freeze for up to 4 months. Roll in parchment paper first.
  • Needed for the cowboy sliders.
