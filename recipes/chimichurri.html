Chimichurri

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Parsley (finely chopped)
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2 Red Chilies (finely chopped)
  • 3/4 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp coarse Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Let sit for at least 2 hours.
  3. Use to baste meats (chicken, steak, or fish) or spoon some on a sandwich or on top of a piece of meat

Notes

  • All amounts are adjustable to your preferences. Adjust it how you like.
